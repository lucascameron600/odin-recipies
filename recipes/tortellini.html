Tortellini De Lucas

Tortellini with red sauce

This tortellini will absolutely knock your socks off. It is a rich blend of italian homemade tortellini, with an absolute stunning array of flavors. This pasta will change the way you get out of bed in the morning

Ingredients

  • Tortellini
  • Red sauce
  • Butter
  • Monterey Jack Cheese

    • Steps

    1. Boil Water and add salt
    2. Add tortellini when boiling
    3. Cook for 8 minutes and strain
    4. Cover with sauce and serve
    5. Enjoy!
